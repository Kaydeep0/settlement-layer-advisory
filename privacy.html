01

What we collect

Settlement Layer Advisory collects information you provide directly when submitting an intake request through our contact form. We do not use tracking pixels, behavioral analytics platforms, or third-party advertising cookies.

The following data is collected via the intake form on this site, hosted by Tally:

Field Purpose Required
Full name To address you in correspondence Yes
Email address To respond to your inquiry Yes
Company or protocol name To understand the entity we are reviewing Yes
Job title or role To direct your inquiry to the right team member No
Description of compliance need To assess whether we can assist and prepare a response Yes

Form submissions are processed by Tally (tally.so). Tally operates under its own privacy policy, available at tally.so/privacy. We receive your submission data directly and store it in our internal intake system.

02

How we use it

Information collected through the intake form is used for one purpose only: to respond to your compliance inquiry.

We do not use your information for marketing. We do not add you to mailing lists. We do not send newsletters, promotional material, or unsolicited follow-up communications. Your information exists in our system only in connection with the inquiry you submitted.

Specifically, submitted data is used to:

Assess your inquiry. We review the details of your submission to determine whether Settlement Layer Advisory can assist with your compliance need and at what scope.

Respond within 48 hours. We use your email address to send a single response to your inquiry. All correspondence is conducted directly between you and a member of our team. No automated sequences are used.

Maintain an engagement record. If an engagement proceeds, we retain a record of the inquiry and correspondence as part of the client file, consistent with our professional obligations as a licensed compliance advisory firm.

03

Not sold to third parties

Your personal information is never sold, rented, licensed, or otherwise transferred to third parties for commercial purposes.

We do not sell your data. Ever. This applies to your name, email address, company affiliation, and the contents of your inquiry. None of this information is shared with advertisers, data brokers, analytics vendors, or any other commercial third party.

The only circumstances under which information may be shared with a third party are:

Service providers necessary to operate the firm. For example, Tally processes form submissions on our behalf. These providers operate under contractual obligations to protect your data and are prohibited from using it for their own purposes.

Legal obligations. If we are required by law, court order, or regulatory authority to disclose information, we will comply. We will notify you of such a requirement where legally permitted to do so.

No other third-party sharing occurs.

04

Data retention

Inquiry data from submissions that do not proceed to an engagement is retained for 90 days, then deleted from our intake system.

Data associated with active or completed client engagements is retained for the duration required by applicable professional and regulatory standards. For licensed securities compliance activities, this period is generally a minimum of six years from the date of last activity.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the address below. Where retention is required by law or professional obligation, we will inform you of the applicable requirement and retain only what is necessary.

05

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of the personal information we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in Section 07. We will respond within 30 days.

Access. You may request a copy of the information we hold in connection with your inquiry or engagement.

Correction. If any information we hold is inaccurate, you may request that we correct it.

Deletion. You may request that we delete your personal information. Where we are not required by law or professional obligation to retain it, we will do so within 30 days of your request.

Objection. You may object to our processing of your information at any time. This will not affect the lawfulness of any processing carried out before your objection.

06

Security

Intake submissions are transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS). Access to submission data within our internal systems is restricted to team members directly involved in reviewing and responding to compliance inquiries.

We do not store payment information. No financial data of any kind is collected through this site.

Regulatory intelligence reports published by Eigenstate Research are timestamped to Base mainnet prior to publication. This is a credibility mechanism, not a data collection mechanism. No personal information is written to the blockchain in connection with this process.

07

Contact us

For any questions about this privacy policy, to request access to your data, or to submit a deletion request, contact us directly:

Data requests and privacy inquiries
privacy@settlementlayeradvisory.com

We respond to all data requests within 30 days.
