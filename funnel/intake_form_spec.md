# Intake Form Specification — Tally.so

Build at: tally.so/new
Form title: Settlement Layer Advisory — Intake

---

## Fields

**1. Full name**
Type: Short text
Required: Yes

**2. Protocol or fund name**
Type: Short text
Required: Yes
Placeholder: "e.g. Ondo Finance, Circle, Franklin Templeton"

**3. What do you need help with?**
Type: Multi-select (checkboxes)
Required: Yes
Options:
- Investor onboarding & accredited investor verification
- Compliance structuring (Reg D / Reg S / tokenized offering)
- Regulatory intelligence briefing (monthly field report)
- Private credit structure analysis
- Other

**4. Current timeline**
Type: Single select (radio)
Required: Yes
Options:
- Immediate (within 30 days)
- 1-3 months
- Just exploring

**5. How did you find Settlement Layer Advisory?**
Type: Single select + Other
Required: No
Options:
- LinkedIn
- Paragraph / Eigenstate Research
- Twitter / X (@GeniusFlow_)
- Referral
- Other (open text)

**6. Anything else we should know?**
Type: Long text
Required: No
Placeholder: "Current regulatory situation, specific deadlines,
jurisdiction questions, anything relevant to your engagement."
